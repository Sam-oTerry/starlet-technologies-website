About Starlet Technologies

Empowering Uganda with Affordable Technology Solutions

Who We Are

Our Story

Starlet Technologies was founded in Uganda with a clear mission: to bridge the digital divide and make professional management systems accessible to schools, clinics, and businesses across the nation.

We understand the unique challenges faced by organizations in Uganda - unreliable internet connectivity, limited budgets, and the need for systems that work in local contexts. That's why we design our solutions with offline capability, affordable pricing, and local support in mind.

Our team is based right here in Uganda, which means we understand local needs, provide quick response times, and offer support in languages our clients are comfortable with. We're not just a technology company - we're your neighbors, committed to your success.

Our Mission

To empower schools, clinics, and businesses across Uganda with affordable, reliable, and easy-to-use technology solutions that improve operations and enable better service delivery to communities.

We believe that every organization, regardless of size or budget, should have access to professional management systems that help them operate efficiently and serve their communities better.

Our Vision

To become the leading provider of affordable technology solutions in Uganda, known for reliability, local expertise, and commitment to client success.

We envision a future where every school, clinic, and business in Uganda has access to digital tools that help them thrive, contributing to the nation's economic and social development.
