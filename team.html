Our Expert Team

Our diverse team combines technical expertise, international experience, and deep understanding of local needs in Uganda. From AI innovation to biomedical engineering, marketing to community engagement, we work together to deliver comprehensive technology solutions.

Mwaka Samuel Terence

Founder & Chief Executive Officer (CEO)

Mwaka is the visionary founder of Starlet Technologies, leading innovation in AI-driven software, digital transformation, and accessible technology solutions across Uganda.

He oversees strategic direction, partnerships, and organizational growth while ensuring all initiatives deliver measurable social impact, operational excellence, and alignment with national development goals.

Lubangaokonya Jimmy

Technical Director – Biomedical & Material Engineering (Japan-Based)

Jimmy brings advanced international expertise in Material Engineering and Biomedical Sciences, gained from studies and research in Japan.

He leads the design, testing, and implementation of robust engineering solutions, with a focus on system architecture, materials innovation, quality assurance, and integrating global best practices into Starlet's digital health and management platforms.

Okello Samuel

Head of Marketing & Software Development

Okello drives marketing strategy, business development, and brand growth, leveraging over 10 years of experience in technology marketing and system administration in Uganda.

He builds strategic partnerships, leads client outreach, and contributes hands-on to software development, troubleshooting, and technical optimization to ensure seamless product delivery.

Okello Emanuel

Finance & Operations Manager

Okello oversees financial planning, budgeting, reporting, and operational efficiency.

His expertise ensures accurate resource allocation, regulatory compliance, data-driven decision-making, and sustainable scaling of Starlet's projects across health, education, and business sectors.

Achan Winnie Babra

Ogwang Allan

Training Specialist
