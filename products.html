School Management System

Complete solution for managing all aspects of school operations, from student records to fee collection, attendance, and academic reporting. Designed specifically for Ugandan schools with offline capability.

Key Features:
  • Student registration and comprehensive records management
  • Automated fee collection and payment tracking with receipts
  • Real-time attendance monitoring with SMS notifications to parents
  • Dynamic timetable creation and management
  • Exam results processing and automated report cards
  • Parent communication portal for accessing student information
  • Library management system with book tracking
  • Staff management and payroll integration
  • Financial reports and analytics dashboard

  • Customizable reports and exports
